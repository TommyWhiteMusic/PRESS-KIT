BIOGRAPHY

Tommy White, born and raised in Carpi, Italy, has rapidly carved his name into the electronic music scene. His journey began at just 12 years old, driven by a deep passion for sound and rhythm. Fueled by relentless determination, he transformed his love for electronic music into an explosive career.

Today, Tommy is known for a unique and boundaryless style, fusing groove, rhythm, and deep musical identity. His sets and productions are loaded with pure energy and emotion, moving crowds and creating unforgettable moments on every dance floor he touches.

He has released music on renowned labels such as Baikonur Recordings, Muted Records, Loser Lab Records, Waveskin Records, Noise Gate Records, receiving strong support from top artists like SOSA, DRAXX (ITA), and many more. His release "Something" on Baikonur Records was officially supported by top DJs including Vanilla Ace, Johan Dresser, Ammo Avenue, Feel Flow, Juanfra Munoz, Mike Ivy, Alex Meireles, Kuestenklatsch & more. Also supported by radios like Fred Dewouters (Warm FM), Jon Hodgson (The Abyss Radio Show), Keef Luv (BeachGrooves), Sandro Dessi (Thoth FM), Si Burtron (Kiss FM AU), Wookieboy (HouseRadio digital).

Having already played in some of the most iconic clubs, Tommy continues to grow, captivating audiences with his talent and unmistakable sound. Whether behind the decks or in the studio, his mission is simple: ignite the party, spread euphoria, and make people dance.
